Sharath Chandra Raparthy

I am a Masters (with thesis) student at the Mila working closely with Prof. Irina Rish. I finished my undergraduate degree from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology where I was a core co-ordinator at IvLabs, a robotics lab at VNIT.

I am broadly interested in reinforcement learning, curriculum learning and game theory. I have been focusing on leveraging game theory tools to formalise the notion of curriculum learning in RL. I recently started gaining interest in independent mechanisms and knowledge factorization.

After my under graduation, I have worked under the supervision of Prof. Liam Paull as a visiting researcher at intersection of reinforcement learning and robotics. During my UG, I have had the privilege of working with Prof. Calogero Maria Oddo, at Neuro-Robotic Touch Laboratory at The BioRobotics Institute , Italy, and with Prof. Torbjorn Dahl at Plymouth University, United Kingdom, as a research intern. I also worked closely with a San Francisco based startup, Gulukul, as a deep learning intern.

As a cool and fun side project, I started jotting down my understanding of the research papers I read and also some cool concepts in math/ML I find interesting. I am planning to publish at least one research paper notes on a weekly basis. You can checkout my project here.

I also find an immense joy in mentoring people . If you are interested please reach out to me. Besides research, I sometimes try to capture random moments.
